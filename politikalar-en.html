QUALITY, FOOD SAFETY, ENVIRONMENT, OCCUPATIONAL HEALTH AND SAFETY POLICY

Our quality and safety standards at Çiftçiler Yağ

As Çiftçiler Yağ Sanayi Tic. Ltd. Şti., we aim to be a leader in the edible oil sector by providing maximum quality service with our trained personnel and advanced technology, producing products to international standards, and presenting them in healthy packaging.

Our company places importance on environmental protection, improvement of environmental conditions, ensuring safe food production, and meeting occupational health and safety requirements in all its activities.

Ensuring customer satisfaction through quality service, effective implementation of Quality, Food Safety, Environment, Occupational Health and Safety Management systems across all process stages, and the identification and prevention of non-conformities form the foundation of our approach.

To ensure the continuity and effective application of these management systems, activities are conducted to enhance employee knowledge and awareness, and management system practices are continuously reviewed and audited.

The Quality, Food Safety, Environment, Occupational Health and Safety Policy is adopted and implemented by all our personnel.

During the implementation of the Management Systems, all current Occupational Health and Safety, Food Safety, Quality, and Environmental legislation will be complied with.
