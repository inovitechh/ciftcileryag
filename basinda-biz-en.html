Hazelnut Oil Consumed During Jewish Passover

Çiftçiler Oil's hazelnut oil, which meets 75% of world hazelnut production, is consumed during Jewish Passover celebrations.

Çiftçiler Oil Industry, which has been producing quality oil since 1965, continues to be the preferred choice for Jewish communities during Passover celebrations. The company's hazelnut oil, produced from Turkish hazelnuts that meet 75% of world production, is consumed in large quantities during this special period.

Quality and Tradition Together

Jewish communities, who are very careful about food consumption during Passover, prefer Çiftçiler Oil's hazelnut oil for its natural and healthy properties. The company's products, which are produced in accordance with international quality standards, are consumed with confidence during religious celebrations.

"We are proud that our hazelnut oil is preferred by Jewish communities during Passover celebrations. This shows the trust in our quality and the international recognition of Turkish hazelnuts."

The company, which exports to many countries around the world, continues to increase its market share in international markets with its quality products. Çiftçiler Oil's hazelnut oil, which is produced using traditional methods combined with modern technology, is preferred not only in Turkey but also in many countries around the world.

International Success

Çiftçiler Oil Industry, which has been operating in Afyonkarahisar Organized Industrial Zone since 1995, continues to grow its export volume every year. The company's products, which are exported to European countries, the Middle East, and many other regions, contribute significantly to Turkey's foreign trade.

The company, which attaches great importance to quality control, produces all its products in accordance with international standards. Çiftçiler Oil's hazelnut oil, which is produced from carefully selected raw materials, is consumed with confidence by consumers in many countries around the world.
