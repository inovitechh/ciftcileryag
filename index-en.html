ABOUT US

ÇİFTÇİLER OIL INDUSTRY has been bringing taste since 1965. Our company, founded by Süleyman ÇİFTÇİ in 1965 in Şuhut District of Afyonkarahisar, was incorporated in 1995 and continues its activities in its facilities located in Afyonkarahisar Organized Industrial Zone.

Our main goal is to process Turkish hazelnuts, which meet 75% of world production, and produce refined hazelnut oil in accordance with Çiftçiler Oil quality standards and offer it to both domestic and foreign consumers.

In our facilities integrated with years of experience and modern technology, we also produce sunflower and corn oil to meet consumer demands.
