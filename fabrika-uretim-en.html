Production Process

The process from raw material to final product

1

Raw Material Acceptance

Quality hazelnuts, sunflower seeds, and corn kernels are carefully selected and accepted into the facility.

2

Cleaning and Sorting

Raw materials are cleaned with modern machines and freed from foreign substances.

3

Roasting and Grinding

Raw materials roasted at controlled temperature are ground and prepared for oil extraction.

4

Oil Extraction

Oil is extracted using natural methods with hydraulic presses.

5

Refinement

Crude oil is refined to bring it to the quality that will be offered to consumers.

6

Quality Control

Quality approval is obtained through laboratory tests.

7

Packaging

It is packaged under hygienic conditions and offered to consumers.
